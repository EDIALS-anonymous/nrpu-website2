Eligibility

Know before you draft the proposal.

A practical eligibility layer reduces incomplete applications and lowers the administrative burden during document screening.

1

Principal Investigator

PI should be a faculty member in a public or private higher education institution in Pakistan, with an eligible academic/professional qualification and appointment duration aligned to the project.

2

Grant load

A PI should hold only one NRPU grant at a time. Prior projects with significant completion delays can affect eligibility.

3

Collaboration

Consortia across departments, universities, researchers, students, and sectoral or industrial collaborators can strengthen the proposal where relevant.

Prototype note: final eligibility wording should be reviewed against the official 2026/27 call before launch.

Eligibility checklist

RequirementApplicant check
PI appointmentFaculty member at an eligible Pakistani HEI; contract duration not shorter than project duration where applicable.
Academic statusRelevant terminal or professional qualification as defined in the current call.

ComplianceEthics, institutional approvals, quotations, budget justification, and ORIC endorsement prepared before submission.
