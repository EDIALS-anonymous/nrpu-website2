About NRPU

Flagship funding for university research excellence.

NRPU supports competitive research grants for high-quality scientific projects with strategic relevance and measurable impact for Pakistani industry, society, and knowledge creation.

Program purpose

The National Research Program for Universities is positioned as an HEC flagship research program for funding high-quality and promising scientific research. It covers both basic and applied research and encourages researchers to formulate their own concepts, methods, and implementation plans.

Funding is awarded to the host higher education institution of the lead academic and administered through the relevant ORIC on campus.

Who the portal serves

Principal Investigators, co-PIs, ORIC teams, department heads, reviewers, and institutional leadership need different views of the same call. This prototype converts static guidance into a structured service experience.

Content strategy

Instead of long text-heavy pages, the content is modular: eligibility checks, application stages, calendar dates, downloads, alerts, and FAQs. That means HEC can update one calendar entry or one document card without rebuilding the entire page.
