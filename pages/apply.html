How to Apply

From idea to compliant submission.

The application journey is split into clear stages: prepare, submit online, screen, correct, review, and award.

Prepare outline

Define problem, objectives, methodology, expected outputs, research team, budget, and strategic relevance.

Submit online

Use the official online portal when the call opens. This prototype can link to RGMS, Monday.com, or a WordPress form.

Administrative check

HEC screens missing or incorrect documents and issues a correction notice during the published window.

Review and award

Shortlisted proposals move through expert review and administrative approval before final selection.

Mock applicant intake

Document readiness

  • Project proposal outline or full proposal template
  • Budget and financial justification
  • PI and team CVs/profiles
  • Institutional/ORIC endorsement
  • Ethics approval or declaration where applicable
  • Industry or sectoral collaboration evidence where applicable
